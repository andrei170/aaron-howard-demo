04 · Process

From first call to cinema-grade ship.

Five steps. Discovery to delivery typically lands in two to four weeks for projects, monthly cadence for retainers. No deck-only engagements, no mystery editing rounds.

01

Discovery call

20 to 30 minutes. We talk about your practice, your audience, your offer, and what you wish your video looked like. No pitch deck, no pressure. You leave with a written sketch of what we'd shoot and how.

Same week
02

Concept and storyboard

I draft the brief, the shot list, the storyboard, and the soundtrack direction. You review and approve. One round of revisions baked in. We lock the concept before a camera comes out of the bag.

Within 7 days of kickoff
03

Production day

I show up with cinema-grade gear, audio, lighting, and a plan. We capture everything in one day for projects, one day per month for retainers. You stay focused on running your business between takes.

Single shoot day
04

Edit, color, and sound

First cut delivered within 7 days. One revision round included. Color grade, sound design, music licensing, captions, and platform-specific cuts all built in. Cinema-grade by default, every time.

7 to 14 days post-shoot
05

Distribution-ready ship

Final files in every format you need. Hero cut for the website, vertical for IG and TikTok, horizontal for YouTube and LinkedIn, square for older feeds. Captions hard-coded where required.

Final delivery
